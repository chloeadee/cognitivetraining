Day 6

Welcome to the second to last day of training!

Today’s new exercise aims to improve your ability to expand your circle of attention.

Aim to complete this training session three times today. You may find that you’re getting better at concentrating, focusing and completing these exercises without distraction. It’s practice makes perfect.

Before pressing play, make sure you find a relatively quiet spot, free of distraction, to allow your full focus on the task. Check in with your posture. You are welcome to sit or stand, and feel free to keep your eyes open softly, or closed. It’s completely up to you.

When you’ve completed the exercise for the third time today, please follow this link to fill out a short questionnaire about your experience.
